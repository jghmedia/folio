Imagine RIT
Poster Design

Imagine RIT is the university's annual celebration of creativity and innovation. This poster was designed to be shown and handed out at the event.

Role Designer
Timeline 4 Weeks
Tools Cinema 4D, Illustrator, Photoshop

After many ideations, I decided to design this poster with the motif: innovation loading. The initial plan was to have the hero text encompass the design and appear to "load" in from a wireframe to a solid. This was the initial plan of the hero text placement after exploring how to implement text in my design.

I wanted this 3D hero text to appear on a virtual wireframe RIT campus, which I mapped out with vectors, as seen in my Illustrator file. I then imported those vectors Cinema 4D to have a top-down reference of RIT's Campus.

Here is a work-in-progress of the initial design

After receiving peer feedback, I decided to go with an isometric view of campus, having the hero text be the centerpiece of RIT's infinity quad. This design received more positive feedback, so I finalized this as my poster design.
