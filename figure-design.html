Minun & Plusle
Figure Design

Minun and Plusle are a beloved Pokémon duo often seen as symbols of friendship and teamwork. This project aimed to create collectible figures that capture their unique charm while maintaining high-quality craftsmanship.

Role Lead Designer
Timeline 2 Weeks
Tools Cinema 4D, Photoshop, Womp

The Process

First, I made reference sketches to aid in the 3D modeling process.

Then I created low-poly models of all of the parts I planned to connect together. I then used subdivision surfaces to smoothen out my models.

After modeling and connecting the entire body, which has many of the same components for each character, I duplicated my model and created and connected all the parts that differed between each models (like this tail seen here, belonging to Minun).

Making each character only one color and transparent made the entire figure easy and cheap to print while still having a polished look.

Utilizing a product photography studio, I shot and edited product pictures for the product's box art
