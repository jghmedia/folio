Moonlight Creamery

Moonlight Creamery is an artisanal ice cream brand that needed a complete visual identity. The goal was to create a brand that felt premium yet approachable, capturing the magic of late-night ice cream indulgence.

Role Creative Director
Timeline 8 Weeks
Tools Illustrator, Photoshop, Cinema 4D

The Challenge

The design system needed to incorporate celestial motifs and playful typography that evoke the feeling of a delightful, welcoming experience while bold colors ensure shelf presence and brand recognition.

The logo system was designed to be flexible across various applications

Multiple variations were created to ensure the brand maintains its identity whether displayed in full color, single color, or reversed out on dark backgrounds.
